Cowsay Random Fortune

A simple script that displays fortunes with cowsay ASCII art.
For a good time call^H^H^H^Hput this in your .bashrc login script.

Dependencies: ruby, cowsay, fortune
Quotes and cows are all cusomizable through fortune and cowsay.
